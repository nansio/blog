Client: 创建2个Peerconnection: 发送，接收media stream

(sender-peerConnection): 用于发送本地videoTrack和audioTrack的local stream

(rcvr-peerConnection): 用于接收其他Client的remote stream

---

Room.createPeer 4 every Client

Peer管理2个Transport, 1负责接收来自Client的媒体流，1负责发送媒体流到Client

===>Example

Room已创建，Peer A（第一视角），Peer B

peerA.createProducer(2) 4 videoTrack and audioTrack(sent from ClientA)

peerA.createConsumer(2) 4 videoTrack and  audioTrack(sent from ClientB)

2producers send media data to every consumer
